<p>Forked from <a href="https://github.com/tanujnotes/Olauncher">Olauncher</a>. Minimal and clean.</p>

<h2>Forked with Extra Features</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Removed clutter, like ads and links.</li>
    <li>You can rename apps in the app-drawer (Renaming apps on the home screen is already supported. Just long-click on an app on the home screen and start typing.)</li>
    <li>We have added a lot more options for gestures on the home screen:
        <ul>
            <li><b>Gestures now include:</b>
                <ul>
                    <li>Double tap</li>
                    <li>Clicking on the clock</li>
                    <li>Clicking on the date</li>
                    <li>Clicking on total usage</li>
                    <li>Short swipe up</li>
                    <li>Short swipe down</li>
                    <li>Short swipe left</li>
                    <li>Short swipe right</li>
                    <li>Long swipe up</li>
                    <li>Long swipe down</li>
                    <li>Long swipe left</li>
                    <li>Long swipe right</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li><b>Possible actions now include:</b>
                <ul>
                    <li>Open specified app</li>
                    <li>Display app list</li>
                    <li>Lock the screen</li>
                    <li>Show recently opened apps</li>
                    <li>Open the notification drawer</li>
                    <li>Open quick settings</li>
                    <li>Power dialog</li>
                    <li>Take a screenshot</li>
                    <li>Switch Page</li>
                    <li>Disable gesture</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li>You can also position the clock independently of the home apps.</li>
    <li>Change alignment of apps in the app-drawer independent from the home screen.</li>
    <li>Change font size of sections of the home screen independently of each other.</li>
    <li>Removed internet permission. You never know what an app developer wants to know about you.</li>
    <li>Lock settings behind biometrics (toggle).</li>
    <li>Added the ability to report crashes via email.</li>
    <li>Added the ability to view app usage time.</li>
</ul>
