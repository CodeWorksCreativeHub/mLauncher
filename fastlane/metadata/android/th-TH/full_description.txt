<h1>Multi Launcher: A Clean, Simple Android Launcher Built for Readability and Control</h1>

<p>Looking for a launcher that ditches the clutter and keeps things easy? <strong>Multi Launcher</strong> is a powerful yet minimalist Android launcher that replaces tiny icons with clear, readable text buttons, making your phone easier to navigate.</p>

<p>Whether you want a simple home screen, need large text for visibility, or prefer quick gesture-based actions, Multi Launcher gives you full control without overwhelming you with options.</p>

<h2>Why Choose Multi Launcher?</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Text-Based Launcher:</strong> No confusing icons — just labeled text buttons for your apps.</li>
    <li><strong>Highly Customizable:</strong> Adjust layout, font sizes, clock position, and more.</li>
    <li><strong>Quick Gestures:</strong> Use taps, swipes, and long swipes to open apps, lock the screen, and access notifications.</li>
    <li><strong>Secure:</strong> Biometric lock for settings and privacy.</li>
    <li><strong>Great for Seniors or Simplicity-Lovers:</strong> Easy to read, use, and customize.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Key Features:</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Custom App Names: Rename apps to match how you think of them.</li>
    <li>Flexible Layouts: Organize apps in the way that makes sense for you.</li>
    <li>Clock & Date Customization: Place widgets wherever you want.</li>
    <li>App Usage Insights: See how much time you spend in each app.</li>
    <li>Gesture Shortcuts: Set custom actions for double taps, swipes, and more.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Accessibility Features:</h2>
<p>This app uses the <strong>AccessibilityService API</strong> to:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Lock the screen with a gesture</li>
    <li>Open the power menu</li>
    <li>Pull down the notification drawer</li>
</ul>

<p><em>Multi Launcher respects your privacy. These features do not collect or share personal data.</em></p>

<p>If you’re tired of flashy, cluttered launchers and want something straightforward, stylish, and empowering, <strong>Multi Launcher</strong> is your perfect home screen solution.</p>
